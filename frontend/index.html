Frequently Asked Questions
How does real-time seat locking work? ▾

When you click a seat, we instantly place a 30-second distributed lock via Redis. No one else can book that seat during checkout. If you don't complete payment, the lock expires automatically and the seat returns to available.

What if two people click the same seat at the same time? ▾

Our Redis SETNX (Set if Not Exists) operation is atomic — only one person can win the lock. The other gets a "seat taken" message instantly. This is the same pattern used by Ticketmaster and Eventbrite.

Can I get a refund? ▾

Yes — refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event. Our payment service processes refunds automatically as a compensating transaction in our distributed booking saga.

Are there service fees? ▾

Yes, a small service fee (10%) and facility charge ($3.50) apply per ticket. These are always shown transparently before you confirm purchase — no hidden fees.
